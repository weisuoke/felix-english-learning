## 8. 【面试必问】如何答出彩

### 问题: "What does [word] mean?" / "Can you explain [word]?"

**❌ 普通回答**:
> "It means [简单定义]."

**✅ 出彩回答**:
> "[Word] means [定义], and it carries a sense of [语义特征].
> It's typically used in [语境], for example: '[例句]'.
> It's different from [近义词] because [关键区别]."

**为什么出彩？**
1. ✅ 给出了清晰定义
2. ✅ 说明了语义特征/语气
3. ✅ 指出了使用语境
4. ✅ 提供了具体例句
5. ✅ 对比了近义词

---

### 问题: "How is [word] different from [similar word]?"

**❌ 普通回答**:
> "They're similar but [word] is more [vague description]."

**✅ 出彩回答**:
> "The key difference is [核心区别].
> [Word] implies [语义特征1], while [similar word] is [语义特征2].
> For example: '[word 例句]' vs '[similar word 例句]'.
> In terms of register, [word] is more [formal/informal] than [similar word]."
